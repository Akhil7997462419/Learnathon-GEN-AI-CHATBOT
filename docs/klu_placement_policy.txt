SECTION 1: ELIGIBILITY FOR CAMPUS PLACEMENTS

1.1 Basic Eligibility
Students must meet the following criteria to register for campus placements:
  (a) CGPA ≥ 6.0 cumulative up to the latest completed semester
  (b) No active backlogs (all papers cleared)
  (c) Minimum 75% attendance in current semester
  (d) Registered with the TPC portal: tpc.kluniversity.in
  (e) Updated resume submitted to TPC at least 15 days before drive

Note: Companies may impose additional criteria (e.g., CGPA ≥ 7.5, no gap year).
Students must check company-specific requirements before applying.

1.2 Placement Registration Process
  Step 1: Log in to the Student TPC Portal (tpc.kluniversity.in)
  Step 2: Complete your profile (academic, projects, skills, achievements)
  Step 3: Upload verified resume (PDF), photo, and certificates
  Step 4: Select desired job roles / domain preferences
  Step 5: Monitor company announcements and register for drives

SECTION 2: PLACEMENT PROCESS OVERVIEW

2.1 Typical Placement Drive Structure
  Round 1 — Online Assessment Test:
    Aptitude: Quantitative, Logical Reasoning, Verbal Ability (60-90 min)
    Technical: DSA, OOP, DBMS, OS, CN (45-60 min)
    Coding: 2-3 programming problems (45-90 min) [for tech companies]

  Round 2 — Group Discussion (GD):
    Topic-based or case-study discussions (15-20 min)
    Evaluates: Communication, leadership, team skills, domain awareness
    (Some companies skip GD and go directly to interviews)

  Round 3 — Technical Interview (TR):
    1-on-1 with company engineers/senior developers
    Topics: DSA, Projects, Core CS subjects, System Design (for senior roles)
    Duration: 30-60 minutes

  Round 4 — HR Interview:
    Salary negotiation (for some companies), cultural fit, situational questions
    Background verification consent, offer rollout

2.2 Important Rules During Placements
  • Students can attend multiple drives but can hold only ONE offer at a time
  • Once a student accepts an offer (signs letter), they are DEBARRED from
    further placement drives for that academic year (Dream Company Policy excluded)
  • "Dream Company" policy: Students with a package < ₹10 LPA may continue
    applying until they get ₹10 LPA or above
  • Offer letter acceptance deadline: 7 days from date of issue
  • TPC must be informed of any direct/off-campus offers for record
